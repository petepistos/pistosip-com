Phishing simulation

Simulations modeled on today's actual attacks.

AI monitors CISA advisories and FBI IC3 threat feeds and generates phishing simulation templates modeled on the attack patterns currently targeting your industry. Your next simulation reflects this week's threats — not last year's templates.

IT security alert

Credential harvesting via urgent IT notification. Simulates the most common vector targeting financial services staff — account lockout, password expiry, system access threats.

Executive wire request

Business Email Compromise simulation targeting accounts payable and finance staff. Models the CEO/CFO impersonation pattern responsible for the majority of wire fraud losses.

DocuSign / document review

Credential phishing via fake document signing request. Exploits the trust users place in document workflow tools — particularly effective against staff handling contracts and agreements.

HR policy notification

Social engineering via fake HR communication — benefits updates, policy acknowledgments, open enrollment. Targets the authority users attribute to internal HR communications.

Vendor payment change

BEC simulation targeting accounts payable — fake vendor banking detail update request. Models the pattern used in the majority of ACH and wire fraud incidents in financial services.

AI-generated custom templates

New templates generated from current threat intelligence. When a new phishing pattern is identified in the wild, a matching simulation template is available within days.

How results become evidence

Simulation results feed Sentinel automatically.

Training completion and phishing simulation results are not stored in a separate system. They land directly in the client's Sentinel record — linked to the CIS controls they satisfy and the regulatory requirements they address.

01

Campaign runs

Phishing simulation deployed to targeted population. Click tracking, credential submission, and report actions all logged with timestamps.

02

Results captured

Pass, fail, and report rates recorded per employee. Remedial training automatically assigned to those who failed within 10 business days.

03

Sentinel updated

Campaign results post to the client's Sentinel record. CIS 14.2, 14.5, and 14.6 evidence status updates automatically. Compliance score recalculates.

04

Examiner-ready record

Dated simulation record, participant list, results, and remediation documentation — all in one place when the examiner asks for training evidence.
