How Sentinel works

From risk finding to audit-ready evidence.

Sentinel connects every layer of the Pistos platform into a single compliance record — from Skopein findings and Manthesis training results to policy documentation and regulatory filings.

01

Control mapping

All 50 controls mapped to applicable CIS v8.1 safeguards, regulatory citations, and required evidence types across every active framework.

02

Evidence collection

Skopein scan results, Manthesis training records, policy documents, and client-submitted evidence all land in the same record — linked to the control they satisfy.

03

Compliance scoring

Every control is scored: Accepted, Partially Implemented, Needs Remediation, or Not Started. Scores roll up to a NY DFS grade and overall compliance percentage.

04

Audit-ready output

CAM, CRB, and board-level reporting generated automatically. When an examiner arrives, the evidence is already organized, dated, and defensible.

Platform capabilities

Built for regulated industry audit requirements.

Every feature in Sentinel is designed around one question: what does an examiner need to see, and can we produce it instantly?

Multi-framework tracking

A single control record maps simultaneously to every applicable framework. Implement once, satisfy NY DFS, HIPAA, GLBA, PCI DSS, and CMMC at the same time.

AI regulatory monitoring

When a regulation amends — NY DFS Part 500, NIST SP 800-171 Rev 3, HIPAA guidance — Sentinel identifies affected controls and flags them for re-assessment automatically.

Evidence repository

Every piece of evidence is linked to the control it satisfies, dated, and stored. Policies, scan results, training records, vendor attestations — all in one place.

Compliance scoring and grading

NY DFS scoring methodology built in. Green, blue, amber, orange, and red grade tiers. Inherent risk weighting. Scoped vs. non-applicable control separation.

CAM and CRB generation

Compliance Assessment Matrix and Compliance Review Board reports generated automatically from live control data. No manual assembly before an audit.

Client evidence portal

Clients submit evidence directly through the Sentinel portal. Submissions link automatically to the relevant control record and update evidence status in real time.

Policy library

15 security policies mapped to all applicable controls and regulatory sections. Policies are maintained current as regulations amend — no manual policy review cycle.

Board-level reporting

Executive summary output suitable for board review, regulatory discussion, and insurance carrier questionnaires. Risk posture in plain language, not technical jargon.
